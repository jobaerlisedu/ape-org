WHO WE ARE

Association for Political Empowerment (APE) is a civic platform dedicated to strengthening democratic participation, political accountability, and environmental & climate governance.

We believe democracy is only possible when political systems reflect the voices of youth, women, and marginalised communities. APE works to bridge the gap between citizens and decision-making spaces by promoting leadership, dialogue, research, and civic engagement.

WHAT WE DO

APE works through four strategic pillars to strengthen inclusive political systems:

Leadership & Capacity Building

We equip youth, women, and marginalised communities with political knowledge, leadership skills, and civic tools to participate effectively in governance and policy processes.

Policy Dialogue & Advocacy

We organise consultations, forums, and engagement platforms that connect citizens, experts, and decision-makers to influence policies and democratic reforms.

Research & Knowledge Generation

We produce policy research, governance studies, manifesto analysis, and evidence-based recommendations to promote accountable and climate-responsive governance.

Civic Mobilisation & Campaigns

We design public awareness campaigns, citizen engagement initiatives, and advocacy actions to strengthen democratic participation and environmental responsibility.

Vision

A democratic, accountable, and climate-responsive society where youth, women, and marginalised communities meaningfully participate in political decision-making.

Our Mission

To advance political empowerment of youth, women, and marginalised communities through civic engagement, policy advocacy, leadership development, and governance reform initiatives.

What makes us different

Unique perspective on political empowerment and climate justice.
